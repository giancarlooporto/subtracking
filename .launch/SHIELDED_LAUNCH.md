## 2. Managing "Glitches" (The Support Buffer)

To avoid personal confrontation, you need a **Support Buffer**.

1.  **Support Email:** All support should go to `support@subtracking.app` (which you can route to your own email).
2.  **Generic Responses:** Never say "I'll fix it." Say **"Our team is investigating"** or **"This has been added to the development queue."** This depersonalizes the work.
3.  **The "Beta" Label:** Even if it's ready, keeping a subtle "Beta" tag or saying "v1.0" in the footer gives you "Grace Period" if a bug appears.

If you don't want to "hustle" on social media at all, focus on **SEO Passive Growth**:
